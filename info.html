Distance Learning A Level Courses Online

Distance learning A-Level Courses offer students around the world the opportunity to study online at home. The distance learning courses are of two years duration and come complete with full online course materials and tutor support via our online learning environment. On completing the course examinations you will receive a certificate awarded by an Awarding Body such as Edexcel, AQA, or OCR

Sports Short Courses

Our Sports Short Courses are intensive programmes where you have the option to complete a Level 3 Diploma course in 10 weeks or less. The courses are all coursework based and split into 10 easy to study and follow units of work. Unlike Accredited Level 3 Diplomas, these Sports Short Courses are fast to achieve and are perfect for people looking to add to their CV or gain a promotion at work quickly.

Accredited Level 6+7 Diploma Courses (ATHE)

These ATHE Level 6 and Level 7 Diplomas in Healthcare Management sit on the Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF). This means that all ATHE Diplomas are recognised by educational bodies and professional bodies at their various levels, providing students with simple progression routes through to university degrees or management roles within Healthcare.

Accredited Level 4 and 5 Diplomas

Our Accredited Level 4 and 5 Diplomas are all equivalent to foundation degrees. These courses are two-years in length, divided into 16 units of study and upon successful completion, come complete with Academic Credits that can be used in support of a placement at university.

A Level Retakes Information

You can choose from either a one or two-year course, and will receive unlimited tutor support through our Campus system. All our courses are studied completely online via home learning. If you are opting to re-take one of your science courses, A Level Biology, A Level Chemistry or A Level Physics, you don’t even have to re-take the practicals, just the written papers.

Legal Diplomas

Management Diplomas
